About Us

Built in Adelaide.
Built for real people.

WenMar Digital is an Adelaide-based software partnership founded by Mark Schmidt and Wendy Pearse — a developer and a GP practice manager who got tired of watching important documents expire, get lost, or never get filed in the first place.

Our products are built local-first. That means your data lives on your device, under your control — not on someone else's server. We believe good software should be simple, honest, and priced fairly.

Every product in the AMBER suite comes from a real problem we've seen first-hand — in practices, in businesses, and in our own lives.
